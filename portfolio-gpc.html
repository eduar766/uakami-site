Case Study: GPC Inc.

Rebranding, website redesign and 360 Online Marketing campaign for Corporate IT Solutions company based in Mexico City.

Branding

Concept and redesign of their new visual identity. Brand identity guideline and overall art direction.

Stationery

Business cards, letterheads and Google Doc templates graphic design.

Web Design

Business cards, letterheads and Google Doc templates graphic design.

Content Marketing & Social Media

Content marketing strategy and social media editorial calendar. Content curation and publishing on GPC’s social media channels Facebook, Twitter y Linkedin.

Sales Presentations

Graphic design of digital presentations for sales team.

GPC Inc. it’s a mexican company specialized in Corporate IT Consulting & Support services. They offer a broad range of solutions including SaaS -like Google Apps- migration and implementation services, server setup and optimization, remote IT support, and infrastructure design and maintenance.

As many other companies in the tech industry, GPC Inc. didn’t have an updated website and a branding that could reflect it current corporate values. Our challenge was to fully redesign its visual identity in order to project their very essence: a contemporary company that uses state of the art tech solutions in order to optimize and facilitate the technological processes of their clients.
